**GPT-3:**

GPT-3 is a third-generation transformer-based language model developed by OpenAI. It was released in 2020 and is considered one of the largest and most powerful language models in the world. GPT-3 has 175 billion parameters, which is significantly larger than its predecessor, GPT-2, with 1.5 billion parameters.

GPT-3 is a multi-task learning model that can perform a wide range of natural language processing (NLP) tasks, such as language translation, text summarization, and question answering. It has been shown to achieve state-of-the-art results in many NLP benchmarks.

**GPT-4:**

GPT-4 is the fourth-generation transformer-based language model developed by OpenAI. It was released in May 2023 and is considered a significant improvement over GPT-3. GPT-4 has 100 billion parameters, which is smaller than GPT-3, but it has been shown to achieve similar or better results in many NLP benchmarks.

GPT-4 is a more general-purpose model than GPT-3, with improved performance in tasks such as:

* Language translation
* Text summarization
* Question answering
* Conversational dialogue

GPT-4 also has improved capabilities in tasks that require reasoning, such as:

* Reading comprehension
* Problem-solving
